tags:: 代词 Pronoun, 限定词

- 物主代词在英语中表示所有权，其根据在句中所做的成分分为**形容词性**和**名词性**。
-
- ## 形容词性
- My
- Your
- His、Her
- Its
- Our
- Their
-
- ## 名词性
- Mine: This book is mine.
- Yours: This book is yours.
- His, Hers: This book is hers.
- Its:The responsibility is its.
- Ours: The victory is ours.
- Theirs: The house is theirs.
